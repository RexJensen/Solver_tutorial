Who This Is For

This course sits at the intersection of game theory, algorithms, and poker. You'll get the most out of it if you have:

1

Basic programming skills

Python experience helps for the companion code, but the interactive demos work with no setup.

2

Comfort with probability

Expected value, probability distributions, and basic linear algebra. Nothing beyond undergraduate level.

3

Curiosity about poker or game theory

Whether you're a poker player wanting to understand solvers, a CS student studying game theory, or just curious how AI plays poker — this course is for you.
